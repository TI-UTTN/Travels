London

London is famous for its art, architecture, food and fashion, some of which it does better than anywhere else in the world.

.01 London Theatre

London has the best theatre scene in the world. It attracts the best in acting talent so don't be surprised to see a few famous faces on the London stage.

.02 Free Attractions

London is home to some of the best museums and galleries in the world - many of which are free.

.03 Sports

See a match in London or take a tour of the capital's spectacular sporting venues from the home of the English national football team Wembley Stadium, to Lord's Cricket Ground or Wimbledon Lawn Tennis Museum.
